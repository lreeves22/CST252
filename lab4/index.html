Task 1: Pseudocode an everyday task

Baking a Cake

  • 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit
  • 2. Pour contents of box cake mix into bowl
  • 3. Add water, eggs, and oil to cake mix
  • 4. Stir until smooth!
  • 5. Find a 8x8 baking circle pan
  • 6. Grease pan with canola oil spray
  • 7. Pour cake mix into baking pan
  • 8. Place into oven and bake for 35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean from the center of cake
  • 9. Place in fridge or out to cool
  • 10. Take your store bought frosting and frost the top with a thick layer once it has cooled

    How to make a bingo game

    • 1. Gather 3 or more people
    • 2. Download a bingo outline
    • 3. Pick out 10 words
    • 4. Make a selection of 5 words of the ten
    • 5. Input /make word bingo covers
    • 6. Have a bingo caller
    • 7. Cover the words called out of the 5
    • 8. Select winner that wins

    • 10. Horizontally
